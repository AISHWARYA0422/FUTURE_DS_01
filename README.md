# FUTURE_DS_01
 A client ready Business Sales Performance Analytics dashboard report with clear insights and actionable recommendations.
 
📊Project Name:

Business Sales Performance Analytics

📌Overview:

This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to understand overall performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends. The goal is to identify key factors affecting sales, track performance across different categories, and support data-driven decision-making using interactive dashboards.

🔧Tools Used:

Power BI – for data visualization and dashboard creation
Microsoft Excel – for data cleaning and preprocessing
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – for calculations and measures
Data Modeling – to build relationships between datasets

🧠Key Insights:

Revenue shows variation across different product categories and time periods
A small number of products contribute to a large portion of total sales (Top performers)
Certain months/periods have higher sales due to seasonal demand
Customer purchasing patterns indicate repeat buying behavior in some segments
Some categories/products have low sales and need improvement

💡Recommendations:

Focus on promoting high-performing products to maximize revenue
Improve marketing strategies for low-performing categories
Offer discounts or bundles during low-sales periods
Analyze customer behavior to increase retention and repeat purchases
Optimize inventory based on demand trends

✅Outcomes of Task 1:

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard for sales analysis
Identified top-performing products and categories
Gained insights into revenue trends and customer behavior
Improved understanding of business performance through data visualization
Provided actionable recommendations for business growth
